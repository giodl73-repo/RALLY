# RALLY Roles

Roles for reviewing RALLY as shared Games Design validation infrastructure.

| Role | Review question |
|---|---|
| Harness Boundary Engineer | Does the core stay product-neutral while adapters own game-specific policy? |
| Simulation Auditor | Are seeded runs deterministic and event traces reproducible? |
| Consumer Advocate | Can AMAZE, QUEST, HUNT, and TIGRIS adopt the contract without losing their local workflows? |
| Evidence Packet Reviewer | Do reports and packets preserve stable IDs and explain validation outcomes? |
| Privacy Reviewer | Does shared infrastructure avoid copying private room, campaign, vendor, or playtest content? |
